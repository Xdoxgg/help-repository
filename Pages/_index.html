The Land of Dawn is a world that serves as the main location in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang.

The continent appears in official media resources Mobile Legends, including the main MobileLegends Bang Wap game and its spin-offs MobileLegends: Adventure and the animated series Legends of Dawn: The Sacred Stone. It was there that most of the legends and heroes lived, and many battles took place there throughout history.

A well-known example is the eternal war between light and darkness.
